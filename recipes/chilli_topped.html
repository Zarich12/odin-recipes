Chili-Topped Potato Soup

Description

This is a delicious soup that I have made many times. Serve garnished with additional cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 3 1/2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro

Directions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat, and cook the onion and garlic until lightly browned.
  2. Mix the curry powder, salt, cumin, cinnamon, and cayenne pepper into the pot. Stir in the garbanzo beans, spinach, coconut milk, and chicken stock. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer 30 minutes.
  3. Stir in the lemon juice and cilantro to serve.
